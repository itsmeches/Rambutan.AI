How It Works

RambutanAI uses an advanced Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on a diverse dataset of rambutan fruits. The model detects signs of ripeness, nutrient deficiencies, and overall fruit condition based on image features.

Step-by-Step Process
  • 📸 Step 1: Upload a clear photo of a rambutan fruit.
  • 🤖 Step 2: The image is analyzed using deep learning algorithms.
  • 📊 Step 3: You receive a condition label (e.g., healthy, ripe).

Tips for Best Results
  • ✅ Use a plain white background or remove background.
  • ✅ Ensure the fruit is centered and visible.
  • ✅ Avoid blurry or cluttered images.
  • ✅ Prefer natural light.

These help the AI model focus on the fruit and reduce misclassification.
