How It Works

RambutanAI uses an advanced Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on a diverse dataset of rambutan fruits. The model detects signs of ripeness, nutrient deficiencies, and overall fruit condition based on image features.

Step-by-Step Process

  • 📸 Step 1: Upload a clear photo of a rambutan fruit.
  • 🤖 Step 2: The image is analyzed using deep learning algorithms trained specifically for this task.
  • 📊 Step 3: You receive a prediction label indicating the detected condition (e.g., healthy, nitrogen deficiency, ripe, unripe, etc.).

Tips for Best Results

  • ✅ Use images with a plain white background, or remove the background if possible.
  • ✅ Make sure the fruit is centered and clearly visible.
  • ✅ Avoid blurry, dark, or cluttered images.
  • ✅ Natural lighting is preferred over flash.

These suggestions help the AI model focus on the fruit itself and reduce the chance of misclassification due to noisy or complex backgrounds.
