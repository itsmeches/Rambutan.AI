How RambutanAI Works

RambutanAI RambutanAI is an intelligent image-based system that uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to assess the current state of rambutan fruits—including their ripeness and overall health—based on visual features. Simply upload a photo, and the system provides instant feedback with visual explanations.

Step-by-Step Process
  • 📸 Step 1: Upload a clear photo of a rambutan fruit via the system interface.
  • 🧠 Step 2: The AI removes the background and runs image analysis using a trained CNN model.
  • 📊 Step 3: You receive a predicted label (e.g., Ripe, Raw, Rotten) along with a confidence score.
  • 🔥 Step 4: A visual heatmap highlights which parts of the image influenced the model's decision.

Tips for Best Results
  • ✅ Use a plain or white background if possible (or let the app remove it automatically).
  • ✅ Center the rambutan fruit and keep it well-lit.
  • ✅ Avoid motion blur and background clutter.
  • ✅ Capture images under natural lighting conditions when available.

Following these tips helps the AI model better focus on the fruit, increasing prediction accuracy and reliability.
