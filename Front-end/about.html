About This Project

RambutanAI is a deep learning-powered initiative developed by a group of Computer Science students under the Deep Learning subject.

🎯 Our Goal

Help farmers and researchers assess rambutan fruit health through image classification powered by AI.

🌱 Our Vision

Empower local farmers with accessible tools that enhance productivity, reduce waste, and promote sustainable farming.

🛠 Technologies Used

  • 🔍 Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN)
  • 🖥️ TensorFlow / Keras
  • 🌐 HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • 📸 Image Processing Techniques
