About This Project

RambutanAI is a deep learning-powered project developed by a group of Computer Science students as part of an academic initiative under the subject of Deep Learning.

The goal of this project is to assist farmers, agricultural researchers, and enthusiasts in accurately assessing the health of rambutan fruits through AI-based image classification.

By combining machine learning techniques with practical agricultural insights, we aim to contribute to smarter, data-driven farming practices in tropical agriculture.

Our Vision

Empower local farmers with accessible AI tools that enhance productivity, reduce waste, and promote sustainable farming.
